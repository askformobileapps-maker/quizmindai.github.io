How do I scan a document?

Tap the "Scan Notes" button on the Learn tab, position your document in the camera frame, and tap the capture button. You can scan up to 5 pages at once.

How do I generate a summary?

After scanning or importing a document, tap on it to open the detail view, then tap "Generate Summary". Our AI will create a concise summary in seconds.

How do quizzes work?

Open any document and tap "Generate Quiz". QuizMindAI will create multiple-choice questions based on your content. Answer the questions and get instant feedback on your performance.

Where is my data stored?

All your documents, summaries, and quiz data are stored locally on your device. We don't upload your content to any servers, ensuring your privacy and security.

Can I delete my documents?

Yes! You can delete documents from the Library tab, summaries from My Summaries, and quiz history from the Bookmarks tab. Simply tap the menu icon (‚ãÆ) and select Delete.

How do bookmarks work?

Tap the bookmark icon on any document or summary detail page to save it for quick access. View all your bookmarks in the Bookmarks tab.

Is there a page limit for scanning?

Yes, you can scan up to 5 pages per document to ensure optimal performance and quick processing.

Does QuizMindAI work offline?

Document scanning and viewing work offline. However, AI features like summary generation and quiz creation require an internet connection to process your content.
